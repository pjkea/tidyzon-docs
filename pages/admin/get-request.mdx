## Notes

- `pricing_breakdown` has one entry per vehicle (asset) in the request — multi-vehicle orders will have multiple entries.
- `order` is `null` for requests that timed out, were cancelled before assignment, or are still pending.
- `review` is the customer's rating of the provider. Null until the customer submits a rating after service completion.
- `provider` is `null` until a provider accepts the offer (order created).
