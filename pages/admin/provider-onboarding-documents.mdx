## Notes

- `GET /v1/admin/providers/onboarding/{tidyspid}/documents` is for viewing raw uploaded files.
- `GET /v1/admin/providers/required-documents` is a cross-check — it tells you what's been submitted vs. what's still needed, combining background check + washkit + requirement data.
- `results[]` will be empty for providers who have not yet uploaded any documents.
