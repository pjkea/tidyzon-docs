## Notes

- `top_providers[]` only includes providers with **≥ 5 reviews**, ordered by average rating descending (max 10 entries).
- `average_rating` and `top_providers[]` are computed from the `ratings` table (`recipient_side = 'provider'`).
- `new_providers_last_30_days` is populated when no date range is supplied; `new_providers_in_range` is populated instead when `startdate` + `enddate` are both present.
