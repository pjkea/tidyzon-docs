## Notes

- Store `id_token` in `sessionStorage` for the session. Do not persist in `localStorage`.
- `id_token` expires in `expires_in` seconds (typically 1 hour). Call `POST /v1/admin/auth/refresh` with the `refresh_token` to get a new one without re-entering credentials.
- Only accounts with `roleid = 4` (Admin) or `roleid = 5` (Super Admin) can authenticate here.

## Notes

- OTPs expire after 10 minutes.
- After a successful reset, the admin must log in again via `POST /v1/admin/auth/login` — existing tokens are invalidated.
