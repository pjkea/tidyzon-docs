## Notes

- `GET /v1/admin/service-types` returns a flat array, not the standard `{ data: [...], pagination: {...} }` envelope.
- `providerCount` reflects providers who have this type assigned in their active mode-of-operation.
- Toggle (deactivate) is preferred over delete — it preserves historical request data.
