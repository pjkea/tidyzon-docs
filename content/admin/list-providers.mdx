## Notes

- The dashboard pulls `?page=1&limit=500` and computes the donut chart legend client-side. The dedicated `/v1/admin/providers/summary` endpoint exists but is not consumed by the live UI.
- SP Live Locations calls `?page=1&limit=5000` to fetch every provider.
- `avg_rating` is `"new"` (string) when no ratings exist — coerce before numeric display.
